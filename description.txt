CalamityOverhaul Mod is designed to comprehensively optimize various aspects of the CalamityMod. 
The current version has undergone a redesign of over a hundred CalamityMod weapons, making them more refined, practical, and visually striking. 

Additionally, adjustments have been made to hundreds of elements in the CalamityMod, 
introducing more craftable recipes and implementing extensive numerical balancing to enhance the overall smoothness of the gaming experience.

The mod introduces several new extended weapons along with a brand-new crafting system to further enrich the gameplay flow. 
Future updates will continue to reset a substantial number of weapons and mechanics within the CalamityMod, offering players a fresh and novel Calamity experience.
